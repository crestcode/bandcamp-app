About The App

Music Discovery App is a web scraping tool for Bandcamp music release pages. Since they stopped handing out developer keys over a year ago, I had to come up with another way to construct embedded music players automatically with only a Bandcamp URL. After completing the original task, I decided to keep going and grab additional page data like tags and incorporate it into a music discovery app. Happy music hunting!! ♫♫
